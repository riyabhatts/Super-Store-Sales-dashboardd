# Super-Store-Sales-dashboardd

1. Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Super Store Sales Dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer segments, and shipping trends across different regions and time periods. The dashboard converts raw transactional data into clear, actionable insights using visual analytics.

2. Objectives

- Analyze overall sales, profit, quantity sold, and delivery performance
- Identify top-performing regions, categories, and sub-categories
- Understand monthly sales and profit trends
- Analyze customer behavior based on segment, payment mode, and ship mode
- Support data-driven business decision-making

3. Tools & Technologies

- Power BI – Data visualization and dashboard development
- SQL – Data extraction and transformation
- Excel / CSV – Data source

4. Key Metrics (KPIs)

- Total Sales: 2M
- Total Quantity Sold: 22K
- Total Profit: 175.26K
- Average Delivery Time: 3.93 days

5. Dashboard Features & Insights

>Sales Analysis

- Monthly sales comparison for 2019 and 2020
- Higher sales observed in the later months of the year
- West and East regions contribute significantly to overall sales

>Profit Analysis

- Monthly profit trends show seasonal variation
- Highest profit values recorded in the fourth quarter

>Category & Sub-Category Performance

- Office Supplies is the highest revenue-generating category
- Phones, Chairs, and Binders are top-performing sub-categories

> Customer Segmentation

- Consumer segment accounts for the largest share of sales
- Corporate and Home Office segments follow

> Payment Mode & Shipping Insights

- Cash on Delivery (COD) is the most preferred payment method
- Standard Class is the most commonly used shipping mode

> Geographic Analysis

- State-wise sales and profit distribution visualized using a map
- Helps identify high-performing and low-performing states

6. Business Use Cases

- Identifying profitable products and categories
- Optimizing shipping methods and delivery timelines
- Improving inventory planning based on demand trends
- Supporting strategic sales and marketing decisions
